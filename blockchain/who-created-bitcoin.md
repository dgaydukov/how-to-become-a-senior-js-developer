### Technology List

Bitcoin brought a whole set of ideas into one technology

* Novel cryptography
* Peering network (custom peer-to-peer protocol)
* PoW (proof of work) concept
* special purpose scripting language
* UTXO (unspent transaction output) model
* Merkle tree
* Mining fee
